Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to change my website?

No. We convert or wrap your current site into a native Android app. You keep your CMS & content workflows.

Is the app on Play Store?

Distribution can be direct (APK) or via alternative stores. We’ll advise the best route per niche.

How are push notifications delivered?

We integrate a push relay compatible with your app layer, segment users, and set schedules you can edit anytime.

Is this compliant for my niche?

We provide technology + neutral copy. You are responsible for usage within local laws and platform policies.

Can I track installs and events?

Yes. Landing + app expose hooks for client-side or server-side pipelines (CAPI/Pixels, etc.).

How fast can we go live?

Typically days once brand assets and URLs are approved. We’ve optimized for speed without sacrificing quality.
